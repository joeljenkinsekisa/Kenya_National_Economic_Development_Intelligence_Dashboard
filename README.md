# Kenya National Economic & Development Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Kenya’s national development performance using World Bank Open Data. The goal was to transform raw development indicators into an executive-level Power BI dashboard that supports economic analysis, social development monitoring, digital transformation tracking, and national scorecard reporting.

The final solution contains a 5-page Power BI dashboard supported by Python data preparation, feature engineering, composite scoring, and executive reporting.

## Methodology

The project followed the full analytics workflow:

1. Data collection from World Bank Open Data
2. Data cleaning and transformation in Python
3. Conversion of wide-format time-series data into long-format analytics tables
4. Indicator selection and categorization
5. KPI engineering
6. Composite score development
7. Power BI dashboard design
8. Executive insight generation
9. Final reporting and recommendations

---

## Key Insights

- Kenya achieved a National Development Score of 63.08, classified as a Good development outlook.
- Social Development is Kenya’s strongest pillar, scoring 71.96.
- Digital Infrastructure shows solid progress, scoring 63.14.
- Economic Performance is the weakest pillar, scoring 56.38.
- Electricity access has improved significantly, but internet usage remains much lower.
- Kenya’s strongest opportunity is converting social and infrastructure progress into stronger economic growth.

---

## Executive Recommendations

- Boost economic growth and private investment.
- Sustain education and healthcare gains.
- Expand affordable internet access.
- Strengthen digital service delivery.
- Align development spending with growth priorities.
